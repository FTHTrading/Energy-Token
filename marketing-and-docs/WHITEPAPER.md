# Unykorn Energy Token (UNYE)
## The Verifiable DePIN Solar Ecosystem

**Unykorn Energy Token (UNYE)** is a Chainlink-verified DePIN asset that mints rewards as solar energy is generated.

### Core Tokenomics
- **Max Supply**: 250,000,000 UNYE
- **Genesis Treasury**: 50,000,000 UNYE (20%)
- **Dynamic Minting**: 1 UNYE per 5 kWh (Chainlink verified)
- **Governance**: DAO + 48h timelock

### Stack
- Chainlink Functions → SolarEdge/Enphase telemetry
- Solar Setup NFT → hardware registry
- P2P Energy Escrow → neighbor trading
- Foundry-tested Solidity 0.8.24
